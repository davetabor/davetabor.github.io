Whether you are an experienced woodcarver or just interested in learning to carve, if you enjoy transforming a block of wood into a piece of art, we would like to invite you to become a member of the Green Mountain Woodcarvers. We meet monthly Carve-Ins held at various members' homes all over Vermont, to carve, talk, eat and of course carve. We are a great group of people who love to help each other and are always willing to assist when any of us are stuck and need a push in the right direction. Whether you are a beginner or accomplished carver, all are welcome. Our members' specialties range from caricatures, birds, and chip carving, to gargoyles and relief carving. One of the advantages of membership and participation in the Carve-ins is sharing and learning from other members.

In addition to our monthly carve-ins, we have an annual show and exhibit held in Morrisville, Vermont in August. Prior to the Show, we offer a three-day carving class with an accomplished teacher. We are always looking for volunteers to help with the show and banquet.

Our bi-monthly newsletter, "News & Notes" keeps you up to date on upcoming events and reports on carve-ins and meetings.

Annual membership is only $15.
